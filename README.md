## Project Overview
<p>
This project presents an interactive retail analytics dashboard built using Power BI to monitor business performance across sales, profit, customers, products, and regions.
</p>

<p>
The report transforms transactional data into actionable insights through KPI tracking, category analysis, regional comparisons, and time-based profit trends. The dashboard uses data modeling and DAX measures to generate meaningful business metrics. All visuals dynamically update based on user selections through slicers and cross-filtering.
</p>

<p>
This dashboard supports data-driven decision-making through clear visual storytelling and intuitive navigation.
</p>

## Business Objectives
<ul>
<li>Track overall sales and profitability</li>
<li>Analyze product category contribution</li>
<li>Monitor monthly profit trends</li>
<li>Evaluate customer purchasing behavior</li>
<li>Compare performance across regions</li>
<li>Understand payment method preferences</li>
</ul>

## Key Performance Indicators
<ul>
<li>Total Sales Amount</li>
<li>Total Profit</li>
<li>Total Quantity Sold</li>
<li>Average Order Value (AOV)</li>
</ul>

<p>All KPIs dynamically update based on selected filters.</p>

## Dashboard Insights

### Regional Analysis
<p>
Sales performance across multiple states and cities with dynamic filtering for real-time comparison.
</p>

### Category Performance
<p>
Distribution of quantity sold across major product categories to identify high-demand segments.
</p>

### Payment Mode Analysis
<p>
Comparison of customer payment preferences including COD, UPI, Credit Card, Debit Card, and EMI.
</p>

### Time-Based Profit Trends
<p>
Monthly profit analysis to identify seasonal patterns and performance fluctuations.
</p>

### Product Profitability
<p>
Sub-category level profit comparison to identify top and low performing product groups.
</p>

### Customer Insights
<p>
Top customers identified based on purchase value contribution.
</p>
